Overview

Simon is a powerful, emotionally guarded Duke shaped by childhood neglect and trauma.

Personality

Brooding, loyal, witty, deeply wounded but honorable.

Season 1 Arc (Spoilers)

Simon enters a fake relationship with Daphne. He falls in love but refuses children due to a vow made in anger against his abusive father.

After marriage, conflict arises over honesty. Simon ultimately confronts his trauma and chooses love over pride.

Legacy

Simon’s arc proves that emotional healing is possible through vulnerability.
