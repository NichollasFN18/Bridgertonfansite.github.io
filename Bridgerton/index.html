What Is Bridgerton?

Bridgerton is a dramatic romance set in early 19th-century London, following the powerful Bridgerton family as they navigate high society, love, and reputation. Every whispered secret can change a life.

The Story

Each season focuses on one sibling’s journey toward love — from fake courtships to forbidden desire, heartbreak, and healing.

The Scandal

A mysterious writer known as Lady Whistledown reveals secrets that threaten even the most powerful families.

The Society

Regency London is ruled by elegance, titles, and ruthless social rules where reputation is everything.
